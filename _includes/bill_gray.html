Dr. Gray passed away on April 16, 2016. He spent over 60 years in the field of meteorology and made vast contributions in the field of tropical cyclone research. Not only was he a phenomenal scientist, he was an excellent advisor to his 80+ graduate students, an amazing mentor and a great friend.
